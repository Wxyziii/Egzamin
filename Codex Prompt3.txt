5. Create or update Apache Kerberos config.
   Create:
   /etc/apache2/conf-available/helpdesk-kerberos.conf

Content:

<Location "/cgi-bin/ad-bootstrap">
AuthType GSSAPI
AuthName "Helpdesk Kerberos Login"
GssapiCredStore keytab:/etc/apache2/helpdesk.keytab
GssapiLocalName On
GssapiSSLonly Off
Require valid-user </Location>

* Protect only /cgi-bin/ad-bootstrap.
* Do not protect /cgi-bin/ad-login, because manual AD login must remain available as fallback.
